Securing Your Account: The Importance of Providing Your Phone Number

Providing your phone number for getting you started on your Soll journey is a crucial step towards securing your account. It enables us to implement two-factor authentication, ensuring that any access or changes to your account are authorized by you. This added layer of security helps to protect your personal information and financial transactions from unauthorized users.

Your phone number also allows us to send you timely notifications about your account activity, keeping you informed and in control of your account at all times. We are committed to maintaining the highest level of security and privacy for our users. Rest assured, your personal data will remain confidential and will not be shared or sold to third parties.
